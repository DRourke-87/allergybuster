How it works

AllergyBuster gives you a daily pollen forecast for your exact location — and unlike generic weather apps, it actually learns how you respond to pollen over time.

  1. 1

    Check the forecast

    Open the app for a daily pollen risk score tailored to your location.

  2. 2

    Tell it how you felt

    One tap: Fine, Mild, or Bad. That’s all the input it ever needs.

  3. 3

    It learns you

    Bayesian statistics quietly build a personal sensitivity model for grass, birch, alder and more.

  4. 4

    Advice gets personal

    After around 30 days, the recommendations are tailored to your triggers — not just the weather.

Learn your allergies in 30 days

Every day, AllergyBuster pairs the local pollen mix with how you felt outdoors. After a month of one-tap check-ins it knows whether birch wrecks you, whether grass is fine, and whether alder is the real culprit on those mystery-bad days.

  • Morning alert so you can plan your day before you head out
  • Status-bar card showing the current pollen level all day
  • Automatic location detection — always relevant
  • Forecasts powered by the Open-Meteo air-quality API
